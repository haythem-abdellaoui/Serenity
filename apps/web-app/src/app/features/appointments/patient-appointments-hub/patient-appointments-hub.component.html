Common questions

How does confirmation work?

When you request a time, it may stay pending until your doctor confirms it. You’ll see the status on this page and in your full list. For video visits, the meeting link is usually available after confirmation.

What’s the difference between video and in person?

Video visits use a secure link (shown in the appointment) once confirmed. In person means you’ll go to the clinic or office at the scheduled time.

Can I change or cancel?

Open the appointment, then use Reschedule or Cancel while the visit is still pending or confirmed.
